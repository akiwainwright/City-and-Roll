About


The Narrative

You and 3 other people have won tickets to have a chance of winning a lifetime of money and freedom. Only one of you will get the reward and the president of this city and he has decided the first one to make it to his building will be winning this prize. Will you be the one to get the reward or will you watch as the others steal your reward?


Mechanics


The number roll

Each player will take it in turns to roll a number each turn, this number is what will determine how many spaces the player will progress on the board. The only controls here are to start the turn and roll the dice, the simplicity of this mechanic is what makes this board game 'the board game for everyone'.


There are some spaces on the board that will have different effects on the player providing either advantages or hindering the players. These are the event spaces and they have the potiential to move the player forwards on the board, move the player backwards on the board and apply a multiplier to the players next roll.


To win the game all you need to do is to get to the end before everyone else
